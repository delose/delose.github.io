Gaby — Spanish conversation practice · offline, on-device iPhone app

Gaby is an offline, on-device Spanish conversation-practice app for iPhone. You speak or type in Spanish, Gaby replies and gently corrects you, and everything runs locally on your device — no account, no sign-up, no tracking.

Frequently asked questions

Why is Gaby downloading something on first launch?

The first time you open Gaby, it downloads its AI language model (~1.8 GB) one time. This is what lets Gaby run fully offline afterward. Use Wi-Fi and keep the app open until the "Downloading Gaby…" banner finishes. After this one-time download, no internet connection is needed.

The download is stuck or failed.

Make sure you're on a stable Wi-Fi connection with enough free storage (~2 GB). Tap Retry on the banner. If it still fails, fully close and reopen the app. The download resumes rather than starting over.

Gaby isn't hearing me / the microphone doesn't work.

Gaby needs microphone and speech-recognition permission to let you practice speaking. Go to Settings › Gaby and enable Microphone and Speech Recognition. Speech is transcribed on your device. You can also type instead of speaking.

Do I need an account or internet connection?

No account, ever. After the one-time model download, Gaby works completely offline. Your conversations never leave your iPhone.

What language does the app appear in?

Gaby follows your iPhone's language for its interface (it ships in 13 languages) and always talks with you in Spanish. To change it, use Settings › General › Language & Region, or set a per-app language in Settings › Gaby.

Sometimes Gaby's Spanish isn't perfect.

The AI runs entirely on your device, so it uses a small model. It's great for everyday conversation practice, but it can occasionally make mistakes or give an odd reply. That's expected — treat it as a friendly practice partner, not a certified tutor.

How do I delete my data?

Everything Gaby stores — your profile, conversations, and preferences — lives only on your device. To erase it all, simply delete the app. There is no server-side copy. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I change the voice or speaking speed?

Open the profile screen and use the Voice and Speaking speed settings to pick a Spanish voice and adjust how fast Gaby speaks.
